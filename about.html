Our Mission

AI Solutions for Sustainable Waste Management

WasteParrot was founded with a single conviction: the world's waste problem is fundamentally a data and intelligence problem. With the right AI, the right drones, and the right team, every city on Earth can become a self-cleaning ecosystem.

We deploy autonomous drone fleets equipped with computer vision and machine learning to locate, collect, sort, and analyze waste — turning a manual, inefficient process into a precise, data-driven operation.

Our Story

From Edmonton to the World

2023
G-Pioneers Founded
CEO Abdujabbor Ahmadjonov founds G-Pioneers in Uzbekistan, Ferghana — mobilizing 50+ volunteers for environmental projects and planting the seed for WasteParrot.
2025
WasteParrot Founded at University of Alberta
Abdujabbor teams up with Dr. Junaid OOsman and Dr. Rafiq Ahmad. The first AI waste-detection model achieves 91% accuracy in university lab trials.
2026
Seed Round · First Deployment
$2M seed funding secured. First commercial drone deployment in Edmonton. Dr. Ahmad receives ASTech Digital Technology Innovation Award.
2028
Series A — $28M · 500+ Drones
Fleet exceeds 10 drones in Edmonton. 2 kg of waste recycled milestone reached.
2026
Future vision. Global Scale, UN Partnership
Named UN Environment Programme innovation partner. Expanding into South America and Sub-Saharan Africa. Next-gen swarm AI in development.
