Technology

LiDAR Pole Mapping & 3D Oblique Imagery

WasteParrot's advanced drone mapping solutions combine LiDAR scanning, oblique photogrammetry, and AI analysis to create precise, georeferenced 3D models of any environment — from landfill sites to coastal areas to industrial facilities.

Our drones capture millions of data points per second, building detailed point clouds that reveal waste volumes, accumulation patterns, and site changes over time with centimeter accuracy.

  • Centimeter-accurate LiDAR point cloud generation
  • Multi-angle oblique photogrammetry
  • AI-powered change detection between surveys
  • Landfill volume calculation and compaction analysis
  • Deliverable in standard GIS formats (LAS, OBJ, GeoTIFF)
  • Regulatory compliance reporting ready
