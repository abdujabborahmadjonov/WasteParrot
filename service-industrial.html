AI-Powered Industrial Compliance Monitoring

WasteParrot's industrial monitoring drones carry a sensor suite developed in collaboration with Dr. Junaid OOsman's research on methane detection and energy-from-waste systems.

Continuous autonomous patrols detect gas leaks, temperature anomalies, unauthorized waste dumping, and compliance violations — generating instant alerts and regulatory-ready evidence packages.

  • CH₄ (methane) detection with ppm-level sensitivity
  • Infrared thermal imaging for hot spot and leak detection
  • Multi-gas sensor array: CO₂, H₂S, VOCs, NH₃
  • Automated regulatory compliance report generation
  • Perimeter intrusion detection for illegal dumping
  • Integration with industrial SCADA systems
