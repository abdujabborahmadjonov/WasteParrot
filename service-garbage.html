Smart Infrastructure

End-to-End Intelligent Waste Collection

Traditional garbage collection runs on fixed schedules — trucks drive the same routes whether bins are full or empty. WasteParrot replaces fixed schedules with dynamic, data-driven operations.

IoT sensors in smart bins send fill-level data in real time. Our Route Optimizer AI plans the most efficient collection path, dispatching drones and trucks only where needed.

  • Smart bin sensors transmitting fill-level in real time
  • AI route optimizer updating every 15 minutes
  • Predictive overflow alerts 4 hours in advance
  • Fleet management dashboard for city operators
  • Integration with existing municipal truck fleets
  • Carbon footprint tracking per collection run
