Every Citizen Becomes a Waste Sensor

WasteParrot's community reporting platform turns every smartphone into a waste detection sensor. Citizens photograph and geo-tag waste issues — our AI triages and prioritizes reports, dispatching drones automatically.

Cities that deploy the community reporting app see illegal dumping incidents drop by 60% within the first year, as real-time drone response creates a powerful deterrent.

  • Mobile app (iOS & Android) for waste reporting with photo + GPS
  • AI auto-triage: severity scoring and priority ranking
  • Automatic drone dispatch to highest-priority reports
  • Citizen dashboard: track your report status in real time
  • City operator portal with heatmap of all active reports
  • Gamification: points and rewards for active community reporters
