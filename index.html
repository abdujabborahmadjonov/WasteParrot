How WasteParrot Works

A seamless three-step cycle powered by computer vision, machine learning, and autonomous drones.

📍
01

Detect & Locate

AI drones scan areas using GPS RTK, LiDAR, and camera arrays. Waste is detected in real time and pinpointed on the live map.

  • GPS RTK + LiDAR mapping
  • Computer vision scanning
  • Real-time location feed

02

Drone swarms navigate autonomously, hover over waste zones, and collect material across 89 categories with 95%+ purity.
