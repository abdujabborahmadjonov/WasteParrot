AI-Guided Coastal Cleanup Operations

WasteParrot's waterproof marine drones can fly over ocean surfaces, beaches, and mangrove forests — mapping plastic density, identifying cleanup priorities, and coordinating ground teams in real time.

AI algorithms correlate drone imagery with ocean current data to predict where waste will accumulate next, enabling proactive rather than reactive coastal management.

  • IP68 waterproof drone hardware for marine environments
  • Aerial plastic density mapping over 500 ha/flight
  • Ocean current integration for predictive accumulation modeling
  • Ground team coordination with real-time cleanup maps
  • Before/after 3D site comparison reports
  • Marine biodiversity impact assessment integration
