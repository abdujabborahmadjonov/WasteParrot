Abdujabbor Ahmadjonov

Chief Executive Officer & Founder, WasteParrot

Abdujabbor Ahmadjonov is a Computer Science student at the University of Alberta, entrepreneur, and technology enthusiast focused on building impactful digital solutions. He is the CEO of WasteParrot, where he works on technology-driven approaches to environmental challenges and sustainable innovation.

Ahmadjonov has demonstrated leadership and technical capability by founding and leading initiatives that unite young innovators, including his role as the founder of G-Pioneers, an organization that mobilized dozens of volunteers for environmental and community-driven projects.

Under his leadership, WasteParrot has grown from a university project into a fleet of 500+ drones operating across 42 cities — recycling over 2.4 million kilograms of waste and reducing municipal CO₂ emissions by 30%.
