Dr. Rafiq Ahmad

Chief Technology Officer, WasteParrot

Dr. Rafiq Ahmad is a distinguished innovator and thought leader in Smart Systems and Technologies, dedicated to transforming industries and enhancing societal well-being through cutting-edge advancements.

Renowned for his impactful contributions, Dr. Ahmad was honored with Edify Magazine's Edmonton Top 40 Under 40 Award (2022) for his trailblazing innovation and streamlined technology solutions that drive meaningful change. In 2023, his exemplary work in digital technology earned him the prestigious Alberta Science and Technology Award (ASTech) for Digital Technology Innovation.

As CTO of WasteParrot, Dr. Ahmad leads the engineering and systems architecture teams, overseeing the design of the drone hardware-software stack, 5G telemetry infrastructure, and the company's city-scale deployment systems.
