Dr. Junaid OOsman

Chief AI Officer & Co-Founder, WasteParrot

Dr. Junaid OOsman is a visionary leader and accomplished expert in Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Waste Management Systems, committed to driving transformative solutions for global environmental challenges.

He is advancing AI-powered waste characterization, sortation, and methane detection technologies. As a PhD graduate from the University of Alberta, he has combined academic excellence with practical innovation, earning accolades for his work in refuse-derived fuel production and energy-from-waste applications.

At WasteParrot, Dr. OOsman leads the AI research division, overseeing the development of the Neural Sorter — the company's flagship 89-category waste classification model — and the drone-mounted computer vision systems that achieve 98% detection accuracy.
