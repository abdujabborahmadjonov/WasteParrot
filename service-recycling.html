The Neural Sorter — 89 Categories, 98% Accuracy

WasteParrot's Neural Sorter is a deep-learning model trained on 4 billion waste images. It integrates via camera units mounted above existing conveyor belts — no facility redesign required.

The system identifies material types and sends picking coordinates to robotic arms with sub-100ms latency, enabling 98% count accuracy even at conveyor speeds of 3 m/s.

  • 89 waste categories: plastics (7 types), metals, glass, organics, e-waste, textiles and more
  • 98% count accuracy at 3 m/s — matching or exceeding human pickers
  • 95%+ output purity — maximizing commodity resale value
  • Real-time picking coordinates for robotic arms (compatible with most brands)
  • Live composition dashboard: waste stream analysis by mass
  • Plug-and-play installation — operational within 2 days
